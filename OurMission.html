Our Mission

45% of women said they do not feel safe walking alone at night compared to 27% of men. “1 in 4 girls in middle-school today will be sexually assaulted or date raped by the time they finish college.”

Our mission is to empower girls physically and psychologically through our training program. We want to transform the lives of students and in particular girls by allowing them to gain control of their lives by learning to defend themselves. Our vision is for our girls to be safer, stronger and more confident, by the end of the training,. We will no longer have to wait for a rescuer, but will become our own heroes
